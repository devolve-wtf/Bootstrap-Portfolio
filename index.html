About kenput3r

Kenput3r is the nickname given to Kenneth Lyerly by his co-workers at Suavecito, Inc. during it's start-up days. Kenneth's initial responsibilities at Suavecito included managing the companies social media accounts, ecommerce site, building blogs, and fixing the often destroyed by user error computers and printers.

Much has happened at Suavecito since it's startup days. The company has grown and thus has the need for additional support of the companies physical and cloud based assets. Kenneth now manages a team of IT professionals including 2 full stack developers and 5 content creators.
